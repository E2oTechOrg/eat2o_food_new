Delivery And Return Policy

This Delivery and Return Policy explains how food orders are delivered and how return-related concerns are handled for our home-made food delivery services. By placing an order, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Delivery Coverage and Timelines

  1. We deliver freshly prepared home-made food within selected serviceable areas only.
  2. Estimated delivery times are provided at checkout and may vary due to traffic or operational factors.
  3. Delivery timelines are calculated from the time the order is confirmed.
  4. Delays caused by weather, road conditions, or unforeseen circumstances are beyond our control.
  5. We strive to ensure timely delivery but do not guarantee exact delivery times.

2. Delivery Conditions

  1. Customers must provide accurate and complete delivery address details while placing an order.
  2. Orders may be canceled if the delivery location is unreachable or incorrect.
  3. The customer or an authorized person must be available to receive the order.
  4. Re-delivery may not be possible if the customer is unavailable at the delivery location.
  5. Special delivery instructions must be clearly mentioned during checkout.

3. Returns and Replacements

  1. Due to the perishable nature of home-made food, returns are generally not accepted.
  2. Replacement requests will be considered only in cases of confirmed quality or order issues.
  3. Requests must be raised immediately upon receiving the order.
  4. Supporting details such as images may be required to process replacement requests.
  5. Replacement approval is subject to internal review and verification.

4. Refund Eligibility

  1. Refunds are applicable only in cases of non-delivery or approved quality concerns.
  2. Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method.
  3. Refund processing time depends on the payment provider or bank policies.
  4. No refunds will be issued for delays caused by customer unavailability.
  5. Refund decisions made by the company are final and binding.

5. Delivery Agreement

  1. By placing an order, you agree to the delivery and return terms outlined in this policy.
  2. Acceptance of delivery implies confirmation of order condition at the time of receipt.
  3. Users must follow the procedure stated for replacements or refunds..
  4. This policy applies to all food orders placed through the website.
  5. The company reserves the right to update this policy at any time.
